Manolayam Health Care Trust

Manolayam Health Care Trust is committed to uplifting vulnerable communities in Needamangalam block, Thiruvarur district. We focus on socio-economic empowerment and peaceful living for women, children, the physically challenged, mentally retarded, and elderly. Our services include special education, early intervention, community support, and assistive devices, along with facilitating access to government benefits to enhance the quality of life for those in need.
